Holistic Sustainable Approach
IN2U health and wellness retreats in both Bali and Australia are designed to enable you to have a 7 day escape in paradise, whilst learning the latest tools, techniques and methods to become your healthiest, happiest self. We are dedicated to sustainable, healthy lifestyle change. Everything we do throughout the week is to set you up for success once you return home. We are not a detox or weight loss retreat, we do not promote extreme measures that are not sustainable, however by adopting the approach your coaches will teach you, weight loss, stress reduction and natural detoxification will be experienced.
 
Expert Coaching
Our expert coaches work to tailor our group sessions based on the health history you provide, they will give you expert one on one advice throughout the seven day retreat and tools to improve your individual lifestyle, exercise routine, nutrition and mind-set. We are 100% focused on helping you become the best version of yourself.
 
Required fitness level
We understand that sometimes retreats can be daunting. We can guarantee you that whether you’ve never exercised in your life or a complete gym junky, our timetable is built to suit your ability and our coaches are industry leaders trained to facilitate group sessions that cater for all levels. We are here to encourage you to improve your life not to train you or push you to your limit.
 
What to expect
Start your morning with an amazing organic tea, followed by yoga or a training session to kick-start your metabolism and get the endorphines flowing. The group meets for a delicious nutritious breakfast where we discuss the days adventure activities and schedule. This may include waterfall hikes, stand up paddle boarding, surfing, white water rafting, mountain hikes, snorkelling, canoeing and more. Your timetable is completely optional so you pick and choose exactly what you want to do each day. Between our amazing training sessions, yoga and adventure activities there is plenty of time for sunshine, relaxation or indulgences in our day spa.
 
One on one coaching
We have now introduced complimentary one on one session with each of our leading coaches. These are 30 minute power sessions to kick-start you on your road to success. It is optional to schedule additional one on one session with any coach you wish at an additional cost starting from US$80 per hour.

Wellness Workshops
Our wellness workshops are designed to educate you about sustainable, healthy lifestyle changes across your nutrition, movement, stress reduction, breathing, food workshops, fat loss techniques and more. Our trainers are industry experts from all over the world that hold a list of qualifications each in their field. We do not use volunteer coaches or trainees. Our point of difference is the transformation we will make to improve your life long-term. That is our commitment.
 
Our locations
Bali Retreat: Set on the beautiful west coast of Bali in Canggu is our luxury boutique resort. You will feel a million miles away in paradise. Private pools, gardens, spas and river views are just some of the scenery you can expect to look out to from your spacious room. We always ensure our locations are set in quiet areas where you can experience the natural beauty of your surroundings. Deep baths, large shower heads and spacious en-suite, our rooms have it all. Whichever room you choose at this resort, you will not be disappointed. Surrounded in lush tropical gardens, a huge pool on the second level with relaxing day beds and sun chairs where you will spend your afternoons chatting to your newfound friends.
 
Australia Retreat: Set on the mid north coast of Australia amongst beautiful white sand beaches, where it’s a typical day to see dolphins riding the waves and amazing Australian wildlife. We have hand picked this gorgeous location just north of Seal Rocks for its beauty; it’s natural surroundings and amazing beaches. Set in a private home which sleeps our groups of up to 15 guests we enjoy an intimate experience together. Luxurious pool, private lounges and just footsteps to a private walk along the beach at anytime. You will experience amazing ocean views like no other.

Culture
You will experience the best that Bali has to offer and be taken to some of the most breathtaking places on the island. We will immerse you in local culture and where possible incorporate local trainers, coaches and healers to ensure you enjoy the amazing energy of this gorgeous island.
 
Nutrition
We are not vegan, raw, vegetarian or any specific dietary group. We believe in bio-individuality and that every person will thrive off a slightly different nutrition plan. Our menu caters for all types of nutritional needs and we welcome anyone with severe intolerance or allergies as our chefs are experts in ensuring you will be well catered for. Our nutritional advice is always based on our belief that you are your best nutritionist. Only you can determine what works best for your body so we aim to give you the tools you need to understand a healthy, wholefoods diet and ensure your giving your body the nutrients it needs to thrive.
 
Wi-Fi
Although we encourage you not to spend too much time on technology, we do have excellent Wi-Fi facilities so you can contact home whenever needed.
 
Optional extras
If you wish to see alternate healers, have additional one on one personal training, nutrition, health coaching, surfing or other sessions you have time in your schedule to arrange as required. These are at an additional rate however all are discounted rates for retreat guests.
 
Day spa treatments
Whether it be onsite or offsite we can arrange any treatments you may wish to have whilst you are in Bali or Australia. If there is a particular treatment you would like to receive, please advise our staff upon booking so we can ensure availability and schedule this into your package.
